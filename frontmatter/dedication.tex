\begin{center}
{\Large\itshape For Dawn}
\end{center}
\vspace{1.6em}
\begin{quote}
Since I met my Dawn I have come to see the world through her, and everything
around me began to wake up---I see dawns everywhere.

I am waking up learning agents at GryAI (\emph{gry} is Danish for \emph{dawn}),
and, having always liked to toy with cosmology and how everything is put
together, I began to shape those musings into a coherent theory. One of its most
important takeaways is this: universes are not born, they are extruded. Each
exists inside a time so brief that a single moment would seem an eternity, and
yet each spans its own forever---like an eternal dawn.

\par\vspace{1.0em}
\itshape Just like my Dawn is forever.
\end{quote}
