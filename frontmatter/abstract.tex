  {\large\bfseries Abstract}
\end{center}
\vspace{0.5em}
\begin{quote}
\noindent Physics is continuous and conservative; singularities and discontinuities are therefore
artifacts of incomplete theory, not features of nature. Applied systematically---to gravitational
collapse, measurement, baryogenesis, the dark sector, and the arrow of time---within Einstein--Cartan
gravity and an infinite quantum vacuum, this single principle yields a complete cosmological framework
with no fine-tuning. Torsion-mediated repulsion replaces the singularity of gravitational collapse
with a bounce that extrudes a child universe across a persistent membrane at its progenitor's horizon.
The central departure from the standard model is that this dawn is \emph{ongoing}: the parent still
feeds matter across our past horizon, and the dark sector is its live, measurable signature---dark
matter the parent's mass still pressing through, dark energy the rate of that inflow---while the
cosmic microwave background is identified with the parent's Hawking radiation. In the same framework,
elementary fermions are torsitons, chiral solitons of the Einstein--Cartan--Dirac equation, whose mass
hierarchy reduces to a single configurational quantity: the sharpness of the soliton's mass-giving
core. A first dynamical lattice calculation resolves this quantity and finds it within the window the
observed charged-lepton spectrum requires, and all Standard-Model matter condenses from one cooling
transition rather than the standard staged sequence. The framework is a synthesis---indebted to
Pop\l{}awski, Penrose, Smolin, and Wiltshire---that is coherent, falsifiable, and offered as work in
progress; throughout, we label what is established, what is natural extension, and what is speculative.
\end{quote}
