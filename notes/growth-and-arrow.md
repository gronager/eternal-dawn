# Two couplings: arrow<->matter, and biased growth -> dark sector

Working note answering two questions raised before propagation:
(1) is the arrow of time set by the filter, with matter and time paired?
(2) do OG/black-hole universes keep growing by biased accretion, and what does
that look like from inside (CMB/dark matter)?

## 1. Arrow of time and matter: co-sourced and CPT-locked, not causally identical

Keep three "arrows" distinct:

- **Thermodynamic arrow** (entropy increases): set by the low-gravitational-
  entropy bounce boundary (low Weyl -> structure -> high Weyl). This is the arrow
  we experience, and it exists *even with zero baryon asymmetry* -- a perfectly
  matter/antimatter-symmetric universe still has a thermodynamic arrow (it is just
  radiation and annihilation). So the arrow is **not caused by the filter.**
- **Microscopic T-violation**: by the CPT theorem, the CP violation the CVE +
  sphaleron baryogenesis uses *is* a T-violation. The matter-making process is
  therefore intrinsically time-asymmetric at the microscopic level.
- **Matter asymmetry** (baryon excess): the out-of-equilibrium product of the
  above at the bounce.

So the relationship is **co-sourcing, not identity**: the *same membrane event*
imprints (a) the thermodynamic arrow (entropy boundary) and (b) the matter
excess (CP=T-violating filter). They are born together at the bounce and point
consistently -- the "future" is both the direction of growing entropy and the
direction in which the matter (not antimatter) excess was laid down.

**The pairing you sensed is real at the supraverse level, via CPT.** The void is
CPT-symmetric, so for every (matter, forward-arrow) universe there is a
(antimatter, backward-arrow) CPT-image partner. A matter universe running forward
and an antimatter universe running backward are *the same object* viewed through
CPT. Hence:

> Matter-sign and time-arrow-sign are locked. The conserved/​symmetric quantity is
> the *product*. Every observer necessarily finds themselves in the
> "matter + forward-time" combination (its CPT mirror, "antimatter + backward",
> is an equally real partner universe that its own inhabitants would also call
> "matter + forward"). Matter and time travel in pairs because observership
> selects the diagonal of the CPT structure.

This is the Sakharov/Boyle--Turok "two-sheeted" intuition, here at the level of
the void's OG ensemble rather than a single Big Bang.

## 2. Biased growth: OG and BPU universes accrete, and we see it as the dark sector

### They grow (BH thermodynamics)

Black holes have negative heat capacity. In a bath, a hole accretes faster than
it Hawking-radiates whenever T_Hawking(M) < T_bath, i.e. above a critical mass

    M_crit = hbar c^3 / (8 pi G k_B T_bath).

Numbers: in today's CMB (2.725 K), M_crit ~ 4.5e22 kg (~0.6 lunar masses) -- so
**every astrophysical black hole is in the growth regime**, accreting more than
it evaporates. In a cold void (say 1e-9 K), M_crit ~ 60 Msun. Either way, the
large holes that host universes grow; only sub-critical holes evaporate. (Aside:
at our universe's own horizon temperature ~2.7e-30 K, M_crit ~ 4.5e52 kg ~ our
mass -- another face of R_s = R_H.)

### The growth is biased (the filter keeps acting)

The Cartasis bounce is a *persistent* interface (Chapters 1-2): matter keeps
falling in over the parent's lifetime. The CVE/parity-violating filter therefore
keeps acting -- the hole preferentially retains one charge/chirality sign of what
it accretes (and of the vacuum pairs it captures), rejecting the other. Two
consequences:

- **Baryogenesis is ongoing, not one-shot.** The asymmetry is topped up
  throughout the parent's life, which *helps the ~41x shortfall* of the
  single-bounce CVE estimate: integrate the filter over the accretion history
  rather than over one crossing.
- **The void condenses onto the big holes (an attractor).** Runaway biased
  accretion is the microphysics behind the Chapter-5 condensation: free energy in
  the void migrates into the growing universe-holes -- the foam's stationary
  state with matter locked inside BH-universes.

### What it looks like from inside (the measurable payoff)

From within a baby universe, matter injected at the membrane appears not as
particles in our 3-space but as gravitational influence from the cosmic boundary
(Chapters 2, 6). So **continuous biased accretion by our parent =>**

- **Dark matter**: the ongoing gravitational influence of parent-side accreted
  mass (of definite, filtered chirality) felt through the membrane -- exactly the
  Chapter-6 "dark matter = parent's matter through Cartasis", now with a *rate*
  (the parent's accretion) behind it.
- **Dark energy**: as the parent grows, the membrane geometry changes, driving
  extra expansion inside. **Dark-energy evolution is the time-derivative of the
  parent's accretion history** -- a direct, measurable diagnostic. If the parent
  is still growing, dark energy is sustained/strengthening; if its environment is
  depleting or it is turning toward evaporation, dark energy weakens. (This is the
  natural place to confront the DESI w0-wa hints, without over-claiming a sign.)

### A dynamic nesting

Because universes grow, the nesting of notes/the-journey.md is **not static**: a
universe's mass, its dark-matter fraction (the depth gauge), and its dark-energy
(parent-growth) signature all evolve over the parent's life. A young BPU in a
vigorously growing OGU sees more injection -> a larger, possibly rising dark
sector; a BPU whose parent has stalled sees a fading one.
